Your Journey

IOI Roadmap

1

ZIO / ZCO

The first step. Clear the Zonal Informatics Olympiad (logic & math) or Zonal Computing Olympiad (basic programming).

2

INOI

Indian National Olympiad in Informatics. Tests your ability to implement efficient algorithms and data structures under time pressure.

3

IOITC

Training Camp. The top 30-40 students nationwide are invited to an intense coding camp to select the Indian team.

★

IOI

Represent India globally! The top 4 students from the camp travel internationally for the ultimate competition.
